Current focus

INDIA HAS THE MOUNTAINS.

India has the geography and the talent to contribute meaningfully to global winter sport, but the ecosystem still lacks consistent visibility, support, and long-term investment.

THIS PROJECT SUPPORTS THAT GROWTH.

By focusing on storytelling, collaboration, and public awareness, the project aims to strengthen recognition for athletes, mountain communities, and the future of the sport in India.
